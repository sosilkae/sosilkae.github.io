Philips UNHCR

Corporate Social Responsibility project that is working to provide UNHCR with a comprehensive guideline about best lighting solutions for refugee camps

Role

User Experience
User Interface
Research

Team

Armina Foroughi
Robbie Sebullen

Currently working on the first official project done by Philips Lighting for UNHCR. The main goal is to design a platform that hosts global guidelines that will be used by UNHCR to set up refugee camps all around the world.

The guidelines will specifically help them choose, install, and maintain the right lighting solution for different camps based on their own local needs, proposing solutions that range from portable lighting to solar lighting systems.
