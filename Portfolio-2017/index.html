My name is Kae and I’m an experience and visual design student born and raised in Vancouver, Canada. I study at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University with a concentration in Design and minor in Print and Digital Publishing.

I’ve always been the type to observe situations and question dialogues before fully dedicating myself to them. Some may say that’s too wary, but I believe there is a certain meditation and prowess in the approach. Looking at design in this way helps me think through those problems, and challenges me to consider how things might work together in unexpected ways. I don’t consider myself an expert (yet), which is why I like to listen

to others first to seek out what really matters and use my understanding and creativity to build those into comprehensive, visual narratives and experiences. If there’s one thing in my learning that has held true to me in my everyday, it’s that I believe finding the slightest insight of importance in what you do is crucial to your work, life, and yourself.
