Melancholy Pin

Illustrated, designed and produced enamel pins—part of the proceeds were donated to the Canadian Mental Health Association.

Role

Design
Production

Assets

Design, Illustration, Production, Print, Brand, Business

The Melancholy Pin is a personal project where I created and designed the brand, managed manufacturing processes, and handled online operations. For marketing and outreach, I used Instagram as the main source of advertising, and launched an e-commerce website starting with pre-orders. At last, I was able to sell the product store-front at ClubCard Printing, Vancouver, Canada.

With over 150 pins sold across the world, 10% of all proceeds from each sale went to donation. With the support from everyone who bought a pin, I was able to contribute nearly $200.00 to the Canadian Mental Health Association.
